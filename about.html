Our Past story

Prisma 2k18, via Instagram

" PRISMA 2023 was a dynamic showcase of talent and unity. With 25+ events, 50+ universities, and 200+ participants, the campus buzzed with energy. From music to dance, art to literature, our diverse community shone. Despite challenges, the organizing committee, faculty, and participants poured their hearts into the event. Jordan Sandhu and Ajay Hooda's performances lit up the stage, creating unforgettable moments. We proudly welcomed guests like Shri. IPS B. Satish Balan and Haryana's Tree Man, Devender Sura. Their wisdom inspired aspiring change-makers. PRISMA 2k23 wasn't just about numbers; it was about friendships, memories, and the pride of a thriving college community. Success echoed in smiles, laughter, and shared experiences, etching its place in our hearts."
